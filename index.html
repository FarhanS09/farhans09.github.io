What is Fantasy Premier League?

FPL, commonly known as Fantasy Premier League is a Fantasy Game made by the Premier League

There are two modes, Fantasy and Fantasy Challenge

The game works by submitting a team weekly with the aim of getting the most points

Points are accumulated by player performances and this can be:

  • Goals
  • Assists
  • Clean Sheets
  • Bonus Points

What are the differences between the two modes?

Fantasy Fantasy Challenge
100 Million budget to spend Not always 100 Million to spend
Aim to get the highest point at the end of the season Aim to get the highest points each week
Transfers roll over until 5 Make a new team each week
Maximum of three players from the same team Restrictions change per week, it is part of the challenge!

How do I participate?

You will first need to sign up from the Official Premier League website which can be found here

Once you have signed up, you will just need to create a team and go from there!
